# alt-J

Syncopated future fantasy wave. Formed in 2007 at University of Leeds . Joe Newman's fragmented vocals over intricate polyrhythms and sampled textures. Drummer Thom Green doesn't use cymbals—only uses the back of a saucepan for hi-hat . Triangle obsession throughout their aesthetic.

## Members
- Joe Newman (guitar/bass/lead vocals)
- Thom Sonny Green (drums)
- Gus Unger-Hamilton (keyboards/vocals)
- Gwil Sainsbury (guitar/bass, 2007-2014)

## Name Origin
Named after the keyboard shortcut Alt-J which produces the delta symbol (∆) on Mac computers, representing mathematical "change" .

## Discography
- *An Awesome Wave* (2012) - Mercury Prize winner
- *This Is All Yours* (2014) - UK #1
- *Relaxer* (2017)
- *The Dream* (2022)
