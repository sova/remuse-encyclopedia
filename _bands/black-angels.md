# The Black Angels

Washed out psych rock. Heavy statiochromia throughout their catalog.

## Members
- Alex Maas (vocals/bass)
- Christian Bland (guitar)
- Stephanie Bailey (drums)
- Jake Garcia (guitar)
- Ramiro Verdooren (multi-instrumentalist)

## Discography
- *Passover* (2006)
- *Directions to See a Ghost* (2008)
- *Phosphene Dream* (2010)
- *Indigo Meadow* (2013)
- *Clear Lake Forest* EP (2014)
- *Death Song* (2017)
- *Wilderness of Mirrors* (2022)
